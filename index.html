Welcome to Whisk & Wander!

What is Whisk & Wander?

This is a journal of my food adventures! "Whisk" represents my love for food and "Wander" represents the exploration of all types of cuisines. This page is a compilation of all the recipes I love, ranging from baked goods to savoury dishes. The "Wander" section are recipes and dishes that I hope to make or try one day!

About Me

Hello! My name is Sami Hoang and I'm currently a junior at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. I'm pursuing a dual degree in UX Design and Communication & Media. I grew up in Vietnam and have always loved trying different food, especially dessert. I even wanted to go to culinary school when I was younger, I don't anymore, but cooking remains a hobby I greatly enjoy. I enjoy cooking and baking for my family and friends, it's also a way to relax and not think about anything else after a long day. When there's an opportunity, I also love going to restaurants and trying new food. I definitely don't have everything down pat but here are some of my favorite recipes that I'd love to share! Hope you enjoy!
